Welcome to Merit Institutions

Merit Arts and Science College was established in the year 2010 by The Merit Educational and Charitable Trust. Merit Arts and Science College is a Co-Educational arts and science college in Tirunelveli, located at Ambasamudram. Merit Arts and Science College is affiliated to Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli. This college is sized with 50 Acres of land. Merit Arts and Science College is recognized by UGC.
